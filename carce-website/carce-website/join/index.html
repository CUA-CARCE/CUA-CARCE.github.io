Why CARCE

What Makes CARCE Different

🔬
World-Class Facilities
180,000 sq ft purpose-built research campus with quantum cleanrooms, robotics testbeds, photonics fabrication, and XR studios.
🤝
Collaborative Culture
Deep cross-institute collaboration, regular seminars, and an open, inclusive culture where the best ideas win regardless of seniority.
🌍
Global Impact
Research that reaches millions — through 48 spin-off companies, policy engagement, open datasets, and open-source code.
💰
Generous Funding
Competitive salaries, generous research budgets, and access to $240M in total research funding across eight institutes.
📚
Mentorship & Growth
Dedicated mentorship from institute directors, structured career development, and a vibrant postdoctoral community.
⚖️
Work-Life Balance
Generous leave policies, flexible working, mental health support, and a culture that respects researchers as whole people.
