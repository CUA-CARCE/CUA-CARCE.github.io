Our Mission

Advancing the Frontier

CARCE exists to push the boundaries of what computing can achieve — through rigorous fundamental research, bold applied engineering, and a steadfast commitment to humanity's benefit.

Our eight institutes and 24 laboratories span the full spectrum of computer engineering — from quantum hardware to climate-aware software — united by shared infrastructure, collaborative culture, and a vision of technology in service of humanity.

Our Values

The Principles We Live By

I.
Scientific Rigor
Every claim subjected to the highest standards of empirical and theoretical scrutiny.
II.
Long-Horizon Thinking
We invest in research that may take decades to bear fruit.
III.
Open Science
Findings, code, and data made freely available wherever possible.
IV.
Human Benefit
Every research programme evaluated against a simple question: does this make things better?
V.
Diversity & Inclusion
The best science emerges from the broadest possible range of perspectives.
VI.
Collaborative Excellence
Breakthroughs emerge at the intersection of disciplines and institutions.
