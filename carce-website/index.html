About CARCE

Advancing the Frontier
of Computing

CARCE is a world-leading multidisciplinary research center that unites engineers, scientists, and visionaries to solve the defining technical challenges of our era — with rigor, integrity, and an unwavering commitment to humanity's benefit.

I.
Fundamental Research
Long-horizon science unconstrained by short-term deliverables
II.
Applied Engineering
Translating breakthroughs into systems that change the world
III.
Industry Partnership
Deep ties with 86 leading technology organizations globally
IV.
Open Science
Committed to transparent, reproducible, freely-shared research
