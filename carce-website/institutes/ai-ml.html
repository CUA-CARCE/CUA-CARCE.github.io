🧠  AI & ML

The Institute for AI & Machine Learning advances the science of intelligent systems — from deep learning theory to safe, aligned AI deployable at civilizational scale. Our three laboratories cover the full spectrum from foundational algorithms to real-world deployment.

Laboratories

Three Specialized Laboratories

Each laboratory operates as an independent research unit with dedicated faculty, postdocs, and PhD students, sharing Institute-wide infrastructure.

🔬
DLF

Deep Learning Foundations Lab

Lead: Dr. Samuel Williams

Theoretical and empirical research on deep neural architectures — transformers, diffusion models, sparse networks, and the mathematics of generalization in high dimensions.

ASAL

AI Safety & Alignment Laboratory

Lead: Dr. Yuki Nakamura

Ensuring that powerful AI systems reliably pursue intended goals. Research spans RLHF, constitutional methods, oversight scalability, and formal specification of human values.

Language, Vision & Multimodal Lab
