Laboratories

Three Specialized Laboratories

Each laboratory operates as an independent research unit with dedicated faculty, postdocs, and PhD students, sharing Institute-wide infrastructure.

🦾
EAM

Embodied AI & Manipulation Laboratory

Lead: Prof. Elena Torres

Learning manipulation policies from demonstration and interaction. Research on dexterous hands, tactile sensing, sim-to-real transfer, and generalizable robotic skills.

AVN

Autonomous Vehicles & Navigation Lab

Lead: Dr. Kenji Yamamoto

Perception, planning, and control for autonomous ground and aerial vehicles. Certification methods for safety-critical autonomy in unstructured environments.
